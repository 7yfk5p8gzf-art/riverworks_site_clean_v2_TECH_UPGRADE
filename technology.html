Technology & Technical Pack

Every Riverworks tank uses the same hidden side-column closed-loop system with surface skimmer and bottom river slot.

Flow concept

  • Right side: black glass technical column with lower inlet and upper skimmer slot.
  • Water flows from right to left through the display, then down to the sump.
  • Return pump sends water back into the right column and into the river slot.

Technical Pack (standard)

  • Pre-defined hole positions and diameters for S / M / L.
  • Standard pipe diameters for drain, emergency and return lines.
  • Recommended baffle heights and sump compartment layout.
  • Black external tech box glass as standard, no extra cost.

External tech box & magnetic cover

  • External technical box made from black painted glass on all S / M / L models.
  • Magnetic, removable furniture panel to hide the pipes on the outside of the column.
  • Clean look for living rooms while keeping all plumbing fully accessible.

Standard lighting recommendation

  • Week Aqua T90 Pro as the single standard light for S and M models.
  • Two Week Aqua T90 Pro units side by side for the L model.
  • Dimmable output so the same lamp works for both high-energy and softer setups.
